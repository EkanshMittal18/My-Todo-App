About MyTodoApp

Designed to Make
Daily Task Management Easier

MyTodoApp is built with a simple idea — helping users organize their daily tasks in a clear and distraction-free way.

Instead of overwhelming users with complex tools, the app focuses on simplicity and ease of use, making task management feel natural and efficient.

Whether you're planning your day, tracking tasks or managing routines, MyTodoApp fits smoothly into your workflow.

Features

Powerful Features
Designed for Productivity

MyTodoApp offers carefully designed features that help you stay organized, focused, and productive every day.

Simple & Clean Interface

A minimal and distraction-free design that helps users focus only on their tasks without unnecessary clutter.

Priority-Based Tasks

Organize your todos using High, Medium, and Low priorities so you always know what needs attention first.

Secure Authentication

Your account is protected using Firebase Authentication, ensuring secure login and safe access to your data.

Real-Time Data Sync

All your tasks are saved and updated in real-time, so nothing is lost even after refreshing the page.

User Profile Management

Manage your profile information easily, including your username and future profile picture support.

Responsive Design

The app works smoothly across different screen sizes, providing a consistent experience on all devices.

Fast & Lightweight

Built with optimized code to ensure fast performance without slowing down your device or browser.
